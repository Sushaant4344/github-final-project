# github-final-project


This project is a Simple Interest Calculator—a lightweight application designed to calculate the interest accrued on a principal amount over a specific period of time. It provides a quick and accurate way to determine the total interest earned or paid based on the standard simple interest formula:
A=P(1+rt)
Where:
A = The total amount (Principal + Interest)
P = The principal amount (the initial sum of money)
r = The annual interest rate (in decimal form)
t = The time the money is invested or borrowed for (in years)
How to Use
Input the Principal: Enter the starting amount of money.
Input the Rate: Enter the annual interest rate (e.g., enter 5 for 5%).
Input the Time: Enter the duration of the investment or loan in years.
Calculate: Click the "Calculate" button to view the interest earned and the final total amount.
